Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How is your approach different from traditional therapy?

I blend Harvard Medical School's neuroscience research with Yale's positive psychology and sacred traditions. This creates a unique transformation framework that addresses both scientific understanding and deeper meaning-making—perfect for high-achievers seeking sustainable change.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients report noticeable shifts within 3-4 sessions. The Mini package provides targeted relief, while the Deep Dive (10 sessions) delivers comprehensive transformation. The VIP program ensures sustained change over 3 months.

Are sessions conducted online or in-person?

All sessions are conducted via secure video conferencing, allowing you to work with me from anywhere in the world. This also provides the flexibility to schedule sessions at times that work best for your timezone.

What if I'm not sure which program is right for me?

That's exactly what the free discovery call is for! We'll discuss your specific situation, goals, and challenges. I'll recommend the best program for your needs, and there's no obligation to commit during the call.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?

I offer a satisfaction guarantee for the first two sessions. If after your second session you feel the coaching isn't right for you, I'll refund the remaining sessions. Your transformation and satisfaction are my top priorities.
